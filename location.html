Where To Find Us

Directions to Penarth Meeting Rooms, Woodlands Place, Penarth CF64 2EX

Penarth meeting rooms are three minutes walk from Penarth rail station. It is about 2 miles or 5-10 minutesâ€™ drive from Llandough Hospital.

  1. Look for the spire of Trinity Methodist Church which is the only spire in town.
  2. There is a mini-roundabout circle on the junction of Stanwell Road, Cornerswell Road and Woodland Place.
  3. Take the Woodland Place exit keeping the church on your right.
  4. 150 yards along the road there are the blue doors of Penarth Meeting Rooms.
  5. The building is behind and at right angles to Trinity Methodist Church. There is street parking but no dedicated car park.
